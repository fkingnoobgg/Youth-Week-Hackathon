General questions

What does 'Homeless Services' do?

The vast majority of homeless people aren't aware of the free services available them. The 'Homeless Services' was created to change this by visually showing the free services available and their locations on the map.

How do I create an account?

You can sign up by clicking on the 'Register' link below the password bar. Enter your information and click on 'Create' when done.

Help! I forgot my password!

If you forgot your password, click on the 'Reset Password' link below the password bar. You will then have to enter your email. You should receive the instructions in your email within 5minutes.

What do the Upvotes and Downvotes do?

The upvote and downvote system was designed to give the user an idea about the quality of service. If a service has a high number of upvotes it means that a lot of people liked it. You can also contribute by upvoting or downvoting.
